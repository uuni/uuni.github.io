UUNI

Design for Good Food

Internationally awarded

At Mino

Uuni is a beautiful collection of earthenware pots used for steam cooking in the oven. Uuni awards this traditional cooking method with a modern shape defined by simplicity, a tasteful brownish-red color and high multifunctional application that meet nowaday’s range of kitchen, cooking and society.

Two sizes allow cooking big and small dishes while the lids, available in ceramic or wood, can be used as plates, trivets or cutting boards. When not used in the oven, red earthenware stays cold when soaked in water – pots work as well for storing cold dishes and raw-ingredients at room temperature. The small pots fit inside the big ones making it compact and easy to store.

Three people -
one philosophy

Anna Kantanen, Lisa Keller and Tiina Leinonen love designing products that speak to one’s senses. Especially cooking and serving that deals with taste and quality, with getting together and sharing, with tradition and culinary fusion attracts them. The focus on table-top culture matched with paying attention to ecological and environmental issues is philosophy and red thread of the Finnish-German design trio.
